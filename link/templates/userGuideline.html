User Guidelines

1

Sign Up

To start using the platform, you'll need to create an account. Click on the "Sign Up" button, fill in your details, and you're all set!

2

Complete Your Profile

Fill out your profile with relevant information. Upload a profile picture to make your account look complete and more engaging.

3

Browse Products

Explore the wide variety of products we offer. Use filters to narrow down your search and find exactly what you're looking for.

Important: Make sure to verify your email after signing up to complete the registration process and gain full access to your account.

4

Add Items to Cart

Once you've found the items you like, add them to your cart. You can review your cart before proceeding to checkout.

5

Checkout

When you're ready to buy, proceed to the checkout page. Add your payment details and place the order.
